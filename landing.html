Our Story

TRIPPIES CLOTHING was founded by Denise King, a proud Sierra Leonean woman with a deep passion for fashion and African heritage. Driven by her love for the vibrant prints and rich traditions of her culture, Denise set out to bridge the gap between timeless African artistry and modern elegance.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted in Sierra Leone, using traditional techniques and high-quality fabrics. We work directly with local artisans to create authentic designs that celebrate West African culture while appealing to a global audience.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Do you ship internationally? 🌍
Yes! We ship worldwide from Sierra Leone. Shipping fees and delivery times vary by destination.
How can I place an order? 🛒
Browse our catalog, then use the inquiry form to contact us about the items you want. We'll guide you through the process personally.
What payment methods do you accept? 💵
We currently process orders via direct communication. We'll provide payment instructions after confirming your order.
Can I return or exchange an item? 📦
Yes, returns are accepted for defective items. Please contact us within 14 days of receiving your order.
How do I contact customer support? ☎️
You can reach us via WhatsApp, email, or the contact form above. We're here to help!
How do I use your website? 💻
Just browse our catalog and if you see something you love, simply message us on WhatsApp, email, or any of our social media channels with what you want. We'll chat with you directly and make the process super easy—no complicated checkout, just real people helping you get what you want!
Do you do custom dresses, wedding dresses, proms, birthdays, etc.? 👗
Absolutely! We love working on custom pieces—weddings, proms, birthdays, you name it. We'll work hand-in-hand with you to bring your vision to life and make sure you get something truly special. Just reach out and let's create something amazing together!
How do I know my size? 📏
If you're unsure about sizing, just send us your measurements or ask for help. We'll guide you through the process and make sure your piece fits perfectly!
How long does it take to receive my order? ⏰
Most orders are ready to ship within 7-14 business days, but custom orders may take a bit longer. We'll keep you updated every step of the way!
Can I see photos of your work? 📸
Absolutely! Check out our Instagram @trippiesclothings for the latest photos, or just ask and we'll send you more examples directly.
