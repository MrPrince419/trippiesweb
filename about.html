Tradition Meets Elegance

Founded by Denise King, Trippies Clothing is a celebration of West African heritage, artistry, and modern style. Based in Sierra Leone, our brand is dedicated to blending timeless African craftsmanship with contemporary fashion, creating pieces that tell a story and inspire confidence in every wearer. Each collection is thoughtfully designed to honor tradition while embracing innovation, making Trippies Clothing a symbol of cultural pride and creative expression.

Our mission is to empower local artisans, support women, and share the beauty of African fabrics and stories with the world. Trippies Clothing stands out for its commitment to ethical production, community impact, and the vibrant spirit woven into every garment. Our Vision

In five years, Denise hopes to see Trippies Clothing become a global African brand. Already doing business in multiple African countries, her next big project is establishing an NGO to help women from her background in Sierra Leone.

Crafted with Heart

Our artisans are talented individuals from all walks of life who believe in Denise's cause and are dedicated to their craft. Recently, Denise has been exploring tie & dye crafts and candle wax, using vibrant colors and wax to create timeless, one-of-a-kind pieces.
