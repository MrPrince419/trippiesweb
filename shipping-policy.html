Shipping Policy

Effective: June 2024

At Trippies Clothing, we are committed to delivering your orders promptly and securely. This policy explains our shipping methods, costs, timelines, and what to do if you have an issue with your order.

1. Shipping Methods

We offer standard and express shipping options. Carriers are selected for reliability and efficiency. International shipping is available to most countries.

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected shipping method. Customs duties or taxes (if any) are the responsibility of the customer.

3. Delivery Times

Standard shipping: 7-14 business days. Express shipping: 3-7 business days. Delivery times may vary by location and due to unforeseen circumstances.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number by email. Use this to monitor your shipment status.

5. Lost or Damaged Items

If your order is lost or arrives damaged, contact us immediately at kingtrippies6@gmail.com. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be posted here, and your continued use of our services means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

For questions about shipping, contact us at kingtrippies6@gmail.com.
